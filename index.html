About JamGlow

Business Description

JamGlow is a modern Jamaican swimwear and accessories brand that brings together style, comfort, and Caribbean spirit. This business offers a range of products including swimsuits, goggles, floaties, surf gear, and beach essentials, designed for everyone who loves the water — from casual swimmers to professionals.

Our goal is to make swimming accessible, fun, and fashionable while promoting a lifestyle of health, confidence, and island pride.

Mission Statement

At JamGlow, our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable swim gear and accessories that combine Caribbean style with comfort and performance. We aim to empower swimmers of all ages to feel confident, protected, and inspired in every splash.

Vision Statement

To become Jamaica’s leading aquatic lifestyle brand, recognized regionally for vibrant designs, eco‑friendly materials, and promoting a love for ocean life.

Slogan

“Dive In. Shine Bright.”
